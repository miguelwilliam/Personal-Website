One thing I really enjoy doing on the internet is hopping from site to site and seeing where it takes me. That's why my favorite part of a personal website is always the links section!! So, here are some interesting, useful, or curious sites I found, along with some that I visit frequently.

  • Yume 2kki

    Multiplayer fan-made sequel to Yume Nikki, runs in the browser and it's super well made.

  • Lospec

    Website full of color palettes, focused on pixel art.

  • CDwORLD

    Human Finny's pixel club/clique focused on CD and album covers. Super interesting!! It has a lot of content, and you can submit your own creations to it too.

  • itch.io

    A very well known indie game portal, with a lot of great free games too. Great to catch some horror games, small games, or to get out of boredom...

  • Vocal Remover

    As the name sugests, it's a vocal remover, powered with AI. Personally, I use it to get some vocals for sampling.

  • Ake's Multifilter

    Range of filters for quick image editing in the browser itself. Very good for making psychedelic edits!! I use it for music covers for Soundcloud. Very cool project from Ake

  • Cameron's World

    A gif web-colagge. A tribute to the old internet, from the early 2000's.

  • comfy box

    A virtual collection of various types of content, from books and articles to music. It is also a forum for discussions, but I use it more for downloading music-related content.

  • Canvas cycle

    A very cool artsy gif visualizer, containing various works from Mark Ferrari.

  • sadgrl.online

    Resources and materials for website creation

  • BDFM

    Generate bitmap-like designs from images you upload with multiple settings and processes for you to choose. Really useful to creativness and designing. (Used to generate the mana-sama pic from the homepage)
